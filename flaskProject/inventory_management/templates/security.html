Security Settings

Manage your security preferences to ensure your account is safe and secure.

Password Change: Update your password to keep your account secure

Changing your password regularly can help prevent unauthorized access to your account. Make sure your new password is strong, containing at least 6 characters with a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols.

Two-Factor Authentication: Add an extra layer of security to your account

Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) enhances security by requiring an additional verification step, typically involving a code sent to your phone or email. Enabling 2FA makes it harder for unauthorized users to access your account.

Security Alerts: Stay informed about suspicious activity on your account

By enabling security alerts, you will receive notifications when there is suspicious activity, such as login attempts from unrecognized devices or locations. These alerts help you respond quickly to protect your account.

Security Questions: Provide additional security for account recovery

Security questions are an extra line of defense to help you recover your account if you forget your password. You can select from predefined questions or create custom ones.

Account Activity: Review recent logins and account access

Check recent activities on your account to spot any unusual or unauthorized logins. Regularly reviewing your account activity can help you identify any suspicious actions.
